Spectrometer Design

On this site I would like to post some stuff about my diploma thesis, where I have learnt the most during my engineering career. I have had opportunity to work on Czech Academy of Sciences and develop optical instrument for scientific processes. Overall work touched all stages of product development, ranging from mechanical design to coding and measurements.

Opto-mechanical Design

Mechanical design performed in Autodesk Inventor. Basically all of the components are CNC machined or 3D printed. Optical design was developed in Zemax Optics Studio and simulation results were later confirmed during resolution testing.

Coding

Modified open-source driver for CCD in Python. Upgraded for continous data acquisition, adopted calibration and read-out features.

Obtained results

Designed spectrometer fulfilled requirement of enabling process control during Reactive Ion Etching. In this picture is GUI of the Python app with spectrum of Mercury lamp.
